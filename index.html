Work With Me

From Boardroom to Ceremony Circle

Fatima Hali - Founder

For two decades, I led teams in corporate boardrooms—strategy, performance, results. I was respected, successful, and exhausted. Beneath the tailored suits and executive presence, I was numb. Disconnected from my body. Running on willpower alone.

The unraveling began quietly. A sound bath in Bali. Cacao ceremony in Guatemala. The first time I danced without needing a reason. My body remembered what my mind had forgotten—that we are not machines to optimize, but beings to honor.

I trained in yoga, sound healing, ecstatic dance facilitation. I became a space-holder for the transformation I'd experienced. I learned that the skills that made me an effective leader—presence, intuition, deep listening—were the same qualities that make ceremony sacred.

Today, I guide women who, like me, have achieved everything society promised would fulfill them—and discovered it's not enough. The high-performers. The over-givers. The women who've forgotten what their own heartbeat sounds like.

My work is simple: I create spaces where you can put down the armor. Where your body can speak. Where softness becomes your greatest strength. Whether through yoga, sound, dance, or ritual, the invitation is always the same:

Come as you are. Feel again. Move again. Return.

Common Questions

I've never done yoga/sound healing/ecstatic dance. Is this for beginners?
Absolutely. Most women who come to me are beginners. My approach is gentle, trauma-informed, and assumes zero experience. The only requirement is curiosity.
What should I bring/wear?
Comfortable clothing you can move in. I provide all props (mats, bolsters, blankets). For cacao ceremonies, bring an open heart and a journal if you like to process through writing.
Are your sessions religious or spiritual?
My work is spiritual but not religious. You don't need any specific beliefs. If you're curious about reconnecting with your body and inner wisdom, you belong here.
Do you offer private sessions?
Yes. Private 1:1 sessions are available for yoga, sound healing, or transformation coaching. These are bespoke experiences designed around your needs. Book a free 20-minute discovery call to explore if we're a fit.
